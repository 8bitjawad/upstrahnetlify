What You Can Do on Upstra

We've built Upstra to be more than just an app on your phone—it's your personal everyday tool, your digital locker room, and your connection to the entire sports world. Here's a closer look at what you can do:

  • Club Connect: Real-time updates from your club so you're always in the loop.
  • Performance Statistics: Track your growth through practices, fitness, and game-day stats.
  • Connect: Chat with teammates or meet new athletes and professionals.

Your Feed

Stay in the loop with everything happening around you. Your personalized feed is where updates, highlights, and stories from your circle come to life—one post at a time. Post a photo, drop a video, or just leave a quick note—it's your space to connect, express, and celebrate the moments that matter.

  • Connect: Keep track of your wins—because let's be honest, it's hard to remember them all on your own.
  • Achievements: Celebrate your growth, your hustle, and every proud moment along the way.
  • Reviews: Send good vibes! Drop a thoughtful note to a teammate or fellow athlete. A kind review can go a long way in boosting confidence and bringing a smile to someone.
